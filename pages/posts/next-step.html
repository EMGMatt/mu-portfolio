The Next Step Realty


The Next Step Realty is a Real Estate Brokerage Firm in New York City that works exclusively with Recent College Graduates seeking their first NYC Apartments.

The Next Step Realty was founded in 2010 and has grown year over year and now includes 3 locations in NYC. I built two separate sites the original at the end of 2012 with a launch in March of 2013 and the 2nd, most current one at the end of 2013 with a launch in May of 2014. It is a completely custom WordPress Theme based on Zurb's awesome Foundation 5 Responsive Framework with a custom social media plugin as well as a custom Sign Up/CRM plugin.

Technology Used

  • HTML/HTML5
  • CSS/CSS3
  • jQuery!/JavaScript
  • PHP & MySQL
  • Zurb Foundation 5 Responsive Framework
  • Custom WordPress Theme
