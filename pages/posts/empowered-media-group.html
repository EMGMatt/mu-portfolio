Empowered Media Group


Empowered Media Group is a Creative Services Firm located in Central New Jersey that specializes in Web & Branding for Businesses.

Empowered Media Group was founded in 2010 by Bryan Angelo. I joined in August of 2012 and soon became the CTO & Lead Web Developer. I built the Empowered website on the WordPress CMS to make updating of content extremely easy. I am currently in the process of redesigning our site completely from scratch.
